Physicists at Work: Beyond the Hollywood Myth Factory

ROBERT OPPENHEIMER

The Bonn-Oppenheimer Equation is on of the most profound equations in physics.

This 1936 image shows Oppenheimer at the University of California, Berkeley.

Faintly, in the background, we can see that he has begun work on his stunning equation.

THE REAL OPPENHEIMER

Contrary to Hollywood legend, working physicists did not conduct research in three-piece suits.

Oppenheimer's trademark brown suit, bespoke hat and, jaunty pipe were calculated to boost his public image.

This authentic document reminds us that behind the scenes, there was a grittier reality to the daily grind.

We, of course, know this is Oppenheimer because of his well-known affection for the color brown.
